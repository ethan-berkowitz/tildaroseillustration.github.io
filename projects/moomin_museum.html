Moomin Museum x Finnish Illustrators Association

In November 2022, i was invited to be the artist-in-residence at the moomin museum (tampere, finland). I spent the month of november hosting world-building workshops, taking part in creative events for children, living in a little red cabin and filling one moleskine sketchbook full of inspiration and experience from the museum.

here are a few of my favorite moments from the finished workshop mural.

my not-so humble home away from home!

the museum guests and i created a collective new world together over the course of four short weeks. i hosted two workshops per week and it was a blast!

while the guests painted and cut out characters, houses, plants, and vehicles i was integrating them into the landscape to form one big cohesive world filled with wonder and excitement!
