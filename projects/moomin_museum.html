Moomin Museum x Finnish Illustrators Association

In November 2022, i was invited to be the artist-in-residence at the moomin museum (tampere, finland). I spent the month of november hosting world-building workshops, taking part in creative events for children, living in a little red cabin and filling one moleskine sketchbook full of inspiration and experience from the museum.
